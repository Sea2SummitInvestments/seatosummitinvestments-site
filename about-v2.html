Founder • Managing Partner
Based in: Southern California (operating footprint across select U.S. markets)

Bio

Luke has invested in real estate since 2009 and focuses on buying in areas with strong upward fundamentals, with positive cashflow from day one.

  • Real estate investing: experience across residential, commercial, and multifamily investments, including active cash-flowing assets and fully realized projects.
  • Business investing: founding investor in Pure Project Brewing (www.pureproject.org).
